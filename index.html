Welcome

Trying out a church for the first time can be an intimidating experience. At LWFC, we are changing that. We realize that people don't want to be recognized while at the same time being able to enjoy exciting, relevant worship. At Lake Washington Fellowship, you will find a non-threatening worship environment where the fellowship is friendly and the teaching is down to earth practical and creative. If you are looking for a refreshing change of pace and a church that is out of the ordinary, come check us out. You will be glad you did.
